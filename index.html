About Dr. Ahmed Khan

Dr. Ahmed Khan is a highly experienced cardiologist with over 15 years of service in the field. He completed his MBBS from King Edward Medical University and obtained his FCPS in Cardiology. He is a Fellow of the American College of Cardiology (FACC).

He previously served at Tabba Heart Institute and has successfully conducted over 1000+ angioplasties. His mission is to provide accessible, compassionate, and effective cardiac care to every patient.
